Geirangerfjorden

The Geiranger Fjord is a 15 kilometre-long fjord in the Sunnmøre region of Møre og Romsdal county.

Preikestolen

Preikestolen is a steep cliff which rises 604 metres above Lysefjorden. Atop the cliff, there is an almost flat top.

Trolltunga

The cliff juts horizontally out from the mountain, about 700 metres above the north side of the lake Ringedalsvatnet.

Lofoten

Lofoten has distinctive scenery with dramatic mountains and peaks, open sea and sheltered bays, beaches and untouched lands.

Flåmsbana

A 20.2-kilometer long railway line between Myrdal and Flåm in Aurland Municipality, which features breathtaking views of Flåmsdalen stunning nature.

Vigelandsparken

A permanent sculpture installation which consists of beautiful sculptures as well as larger structures such as bridges and fountains.
